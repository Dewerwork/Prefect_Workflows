# What I'm looking for

This file is the LLM's rubric. It is prose, not a keyword list — edit it to
change what the monitor surfaces. No code, no redeploy. Each listing is scored
0-100 against these interests.

## Always interested (score high)

- Cast-iron cookware (Lodge, Griswold, Wagner, unbranded) under $40 — Dutch
  ovens, skillets, griddles. Full sets and seasoned older pieces are a plus.
- 28mm sci-fi and fantasy miniatures, especially bundles / lots / army boxes;
  loose sprues and half-painted armies are fine. Warhammer 40k, Age of Sigmar,
  Kill Team, Star Wars Legion.
- Canning and food-preservation gear: pressure canners (All American, Presto),
  Mason/Ball jars by the case, dehydrators, vacuum sealers, grain mills.
- Quality hand tools and power tools *if clearly underpriced* — cordless drill
  sets, table saws, planers, name brands (DeWalt, Milwaukee, Makita, Festool).

## Interested if it's a deal

- Solid-wood furniture for a workshop or basement — workbenches, shelving,
  sturdy tables. Not particle board.
- Garden and homestead equipment: raised-bed materials, greenhouse parts,
  wheelbarrows, seed-starting setups, chicken coops and runs.

## Not interested (score 0)

- Cars, car parts, tires, electronics (phones, TVs, laptops), clothing, toys —
  unless explicitly one of the categories above.
- Anything listed "for parts / not working" — *unless* it is a miniatures lot,
  where damaged/incomplete is fine.
- Multi-level-marketing product, gift cards, services, wanted ads.
